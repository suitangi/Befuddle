Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and quite frankly, capturing storing and doing anything with your data cost too much, takes too much time and effort. This is a free and lightweight game I made for myself and friends.

Web Version

When playing Befuddle on the web (befuddle.xyz), no data is collected. Your gameplay information is stored locally on your device using browser storage. We do not track, analyze, or store any personal information from web players.

Discord Activity Version

When playing Befuddle as a Discord Activity, the following information is necessary for the application to function:

  • Discord UserID - Required to identify your user account within Discord
  • Discord ChannelID - Required to identify where the activity was launched

This information is:

  • Stored on our servers for no more than 24 hours
  • Used solely for launching and operating the Discord application
  • Never given nor sold to third parties

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through our Twitter or by reporting a bug in the main Befuddle application.
